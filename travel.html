How to Reach Tangeda

Travel options and directions to visit our beautiful village

Nearest Railway Station

The closest railway station is Nadikudi, about 16 km from Tangeda. Auto and bus options are available for further travel.

By Road

Tangeda is well connected by road from nearby towns:

  • Dachepalle (Mandal): 14 km
  • Piduguralla: 36 km
  • Narasaraopet (District HQ): 66 km
  • Guntur: 101 km
  • Hyderabad RGI Airport: 214 km

Buses and shared autos are available regularly from nearby towns.
