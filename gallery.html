Sankranthi Gallery

Sankranthi in Tangeda is celebrated with rangoli (muggulu), decorated houses, traditional games, and family gatherings. These photos capture the joy and colours of the harvest festival over the years.

Sankranthi Timeline

  • 2025: Village-wide rangoli competition and cultural night.
  • 2024: Traditional bullock cart procession and kite flying near the fields.
  • 2023: Community lunch at the temple premises and games for children.

Ganesh Chaturthi Gallery

Ganesh Chaturthi brings beautifully crafted idols of Lord Ganesha to streets and homes across Tangeda. The photos show the devotion, decorations, and immersion processions over the years.

Ganesh Chaturthi Timeline

  • 2025: Eco-friendly clay idols and awareness drive on clean immersions.
  • 2024: Evening aarti and cultural programs at the main Ganesh pandal.
  • 2023: Youth-led decoration teams and community prasadam distribution.

Muharram Gallery

Muharram in Tangeda reflects unity and respect across communities. These images show processions, tazias, and gatherings that honour tradition and togetherness.

Muharram Timeline

  • 2025: Peaceful procession through main streets with community participation.
  • 2024: Youth volunteers managing routes, water points, and support.
  • 2023: Traditional tazias prepared by local artisans.
