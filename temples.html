Temples of Tangeda

Spiritual roots and sacred spaces of our village.

About the Temples

Tangeda is home to several ancient temples that reflect the village's deep spiritual heritage. These temples are not just places of worship, but also centers of community, tradition, and local pride.

Sri Rama Temple

One of the oldest temples in the village, Sri Rama Temple is known for its peaceful surroundings and yearly celebrations during Rama Navami.

Shiva Temple by the River

This temple sits beautifully by the Krishna riverbank. It is especially visited during Maha Shivratri, where villagers gather for night-long prayers.

Village Hanuman Temple

Located in the center of Tangeda, this temple is a landmark where villagers gather every Saturday for prayers, bhajans, and blessings.
