Ganesh Chaturthi in Tangeda

During Ganesh Chaturthi, idols of Lord Ganesha are installed at homes and public pandals. Devotees offer prayers, perform aarti, and gather every day for bhajans and cultural programs. The visarjan procession is one of the most awaited events, filled with music, devotion, and emotion.

Muharram in Tangeda

Muharram is observed with deep respect and discipline. Tazias are prepared with great care and carried in processions through the village. People from different communities participate or support, showing the unity and understanding that Tangeda is known for.
