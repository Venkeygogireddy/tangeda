Festivals in Tangeda

Festivals are the heartbeat of Tangeda. They bring families home, unite neighbours, and fill the village with colour, devotion, and joy. From harvest celebrations to spiritual processions, each festival has its own story and traditions followed for generations.

Sankranthi in Tangeda

Sankranthi is one of the most vibrant festivals in Tangeda. Families decorate their houses, draw colourful muggulu, prepare traditional sweets, and gather to thank nature for a good harvest.

Ganesh Chaturthi in Tangeda

Idols of Lord Ganesha are installed at homes and public pandals. Devotees perform aarti and gather for bhajans and cultural programs. The visarjan procession is filled with music and devotion.
