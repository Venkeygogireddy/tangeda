Sankranthi

Sankranthi is one of the most celebrated festivals in Tangeda. Families decorate their homes with colorful rangoli, fly kites, and cook traditional sweets like ariselu and pongal.

Ganesh Chaturthi

Villagers come together to build beautiful clay Ganesh idols and celebrate with music, prayers, and processions. The immersion ceremony is a symbol of unity and devotion.

Muharram

Tangeda embraces all cultures. Muharram is respectfully observed by many villagers through taziyas and peaceful processions, highlighting religious harmony.

Other Celebrations

Ugadi, Dussehra, and Diwali are also celebrated with equal enthusiasm, bringing together people of all ages and backgrounds to enjoy food, prayers, and cultural performances.
