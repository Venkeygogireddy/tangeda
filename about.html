About Tangeda

Tangeda is a historic village nestled in the heart of Andhra Pradesh. Known for its strong cultural roots and vibrant traditions, the village traces its origin back several centuries.

King Anumula Machireddy Garu

The legendary founder of Tangeda, Anumula Machireddy Garu, was a visionary ruler who emphasized self-reliance, spiritual integrity, and education. His legacy still echoes in the temples and stories told by elders.

Village Timeline

  • 1600s: Founding of Tangeda by Anumula Machireddy Garu
  • 1800s: Temples constructed in the village center
  • 1947: Participation in India’s independence movement
  • 1980s: Village school and water system established
  • 2020: Digital initiatives and festival archives began
