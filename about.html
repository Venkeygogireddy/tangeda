Welcome to Tangeda

Tangeda is a village rich in culture, tradition, and natural beauty. Nestled along the river, it has grown from a historic settlement into a vibrant community that still honours its roots and legends.

The village is closely associated with the legacy of The King Anumula Machireddy Garu, whose influence and stories are still remembered with pride by the people of Tangeda. Over generations, families have preserved customs, festivals, and values that keep the spirit of the village alive.

The Heart of the Village

Life in Tangeda revolves around its temples, festivals, fields, and community gatherings. Agriculture, small businesses, and traditional occupations support daily life, while schools and modern facilities help the younger generation move forward without losing connection to their roots.

Journey of Tangeda (Timeline)

  • Early Settlement: Foundations laid under the influence of local rulers and royal families.
  • Temple Development: Construction of key temples that became spiritual and social centres.
  • Community Growth: Expansion of families, farmlands, and local markets around the village core.
  • Modern Era: Introduction of electricity, schools, healthcare, and improved transport.
  • Today: A village that balances heritage with progress, staying connected to people across the world.

What Makes Tangeda Special

Unity in Diversity

People of different backgrounds and beliefs live together with respect, celebrating festivals and important days as one community.

Festivals & Traditions

From Sankranthi and Ganesh Chaturthi to Muharram and local jatara, every festival is an opportunity for families to return, meet, and celebrate together.

Bond with Nature

The river, fields, and greenery around Tangeda are not just scenic, but an important part of daily life, agriculture, and local stories.
