Discover Tangeda

Tangeda is a village rich in culture and history, known for its heritage and community.

History of Tangeda

Tangeda is a village with deep historical roots, believed to be founded centuries ago by the King Anumula Machireddy Garu. Over the years, the village has witnessed cultural transformation, temple developments, and the rise of traditional events that are still celebrated today.

The legacy of the royal family continues to influence the community's respect for values, traditions, and unity.
