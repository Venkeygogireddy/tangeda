Ariselu (Jaggery Rice Sweets)

Ingredients: Rice flour, jaggery, sesame seeds, ghee.

Steps:

  • Boil jaggery with water to make thick syrup.
  • Mix with rice flour to form soft dough.
  • Flatten and fry on low flame with ghee.
  • Sprinkle sesame seeds and serve warm.

Garelu (Medu Vada)

Ingredients: Urad dal, green chilli, onion, cumin.

Steps:

  • Soak and grind urad dal into a fluffy batter.
  • Add chopped onions, chilies, and cumin.
  • Shape into doughnuts and deep fry until golden.

Pongal (Sweet Rice)

Ingredients: Rice, moong dal, jaggery, cardamom, dry fruits.

Steps:

  • Boil rice and dal together until soft.
  • Add melted jaggery, cardamom, and ghee-roasted cashews.
  • Serve hot during Sankranthi or festivals.
