Honoring Our Community

Over the years, Tangeda has been home to many respected teachers, leaders, and social workers who made a difference in people’s lives. Here are a few of them:

Master Krishnaiah Garu

Veteran schoolteacher who educated two generations and helped build the first school building in the village.

Dr. Lakshmi

The first female doctor from Tangeda, known for her service to rural women’s health and maternity care.

Sri Ramachandra Rao

A freedom fighter who participated in local independence movements and led village development campaigns.
